Mike served 10 years in the U.S. Marine Corps, spending 5 years as an artilleryman deploying to Operation Iraqi Freedom twice and 5 years as a Counterintelligence / Human Intelligence Specialist with deployments to Eastern Europe and South America.

After leaving the Marines, Mike worked in digital advertising, leveraging experience from a previous business he had started in Texas. He worked for a large auto group and consulted with various small businesses.

In 2017, Mike started Digital Edge Advertising, a digital advertising agency that ran for 3 years before being asked to join a role that opened due to a client acquisition.

Mike now works at Epsilon building scalable digital advertising programs for the automotive industry, working with internal engineering departments to design solutions.

To augment this role and gain a new skill set, Mike enrolled in and is currently completing the MIT xPro MERN Full Stack Developer Certificate program.
