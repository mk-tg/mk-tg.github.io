Project Title: PacMan

Description: This project utilizes JavaScript and images of PacMan in different states (mouth open and closed and facing left and right) to generate a PacMan that moves from left to right in the bounds of the box. The box resizes based on browser width to support all devices.

How to Run: Click the "Run" button to start PacMan moving from left to right.

Roadmap of Future Improvements:

  • Inputs and buttons for the user to generate additional Pacmen
  • Adding vertical movement
  • Color selection for the background
